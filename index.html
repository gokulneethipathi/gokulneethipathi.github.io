About

From raw events to governed insight

I'm a Senior Data Engineer with 5+ years architecting cloud-native data platforms on Azure and AWS. My focus is production-grade ETL/ELT built on Apache Spark, PySpark, Databricks, Snowflake, and Azure Data Factory, with deep work in Medallion Architecture, dimensional modeling, and lakehouse design.
